Home → About → Getting Here → Accommodations

If you are seeking accommodations during your stay in Boston, please consider the Doubletree Club Hotel at Boston-Bayside which is close to campus. In addition to this hotel, Boston offers a wide range of overnight accommodations. Below is a selected listing.

The following list of accommodations provides variety for those interested in staying in downtown Boston or in close proximity to UMass Boston. 

DoubleTree Club by Hilton Hotel Boston Bayside
Located adjacent to the campus. For reservations, call 617.822.3600 or follow this link to use the UMass Boston corporate rate. Offers free shuttle service to Logan Airport and downtown Boston, and complimentary business center. 240 Mt. Vernon Street, Boston MA 02125. 

Boston Park Plaza Hotel
Located approximately three miles from the university on the Red Line. Please call 1.800.225.2008 and request the UMass Boston preferred rate. 50 Park Plaza at Arlington Street, Boston MA 02116.

Best Western Adams Inn
Featuring discounts for UMass Boston visitors and a complimentary shuttle service to and from Logan Airport and the Seaport. Please call 800.528.1234 for reservations, or 617.328.1500 to call the hotel directly. 29 Hancock Street, Quincy MA 02171. 

Westin Boston Waterfront
The Westin Boston Waterfront Hotel is an AAA Four Diamond award-winner and is less than three miles from Logan International Airport. 425 Summer Street, Boston MA 02210

Hampton Inn
Please call 617.445.6400. Crosstown Center, 811 Massachusetts Avenue, Boston MA 02118. 

Holiday Inn Express Boston
Please call 617.288.3030 and request the UMass Boston corporate rate. 69 Boston Street, Boston MA 02125. 

Comfort Inn Boston
Located adjacent to the campus. Offers a free 24 hour transportation to and from Logan Airport. Please call 617.287.9200. 900 Morrissey Boulevard, Boston MA 02122. 

Courtyard Marriott South Boston
Offers complimentary airport shuttle. Please call 617.436.8200. 63 R Boston Street, Boston MA 02125. 

Flipkey
Flipkey.com is a Boston-based company with vacation rental properties in and around the city. Through Flipkey, you can check rates and availability, read trusted reviews, and even book your stay online. These alternative accommodations are perfect.
